# 1. Executive Summary

This document is the final production blueprint of the iKIA Logistics Platform.

It consolidates all approved Strategic, Business, Application, Data and Solution Architecture artifacts into a single executable implementation model.

This blueprint serves as the master execution document for:

- Executive Committee
- Product Team
- Engineering Team
- AI Team
- DevOps Team
- Operations Team
- Investors
- Government Stakeholders

---

# 2. Strategic Objective

Build the National Digital Logistics Operating System of Iran capable of:

- Logistics Digitalization
- Supply Chain Orchestration
- Trade Enablement
- AI-Powered Decision Support
- Corridor Intelligence
- National Logistics Visibility
- International Expansion
